Western Carolina Waterscapes owner Art Lynch is the premier water-feature and pond design expert in Western Carolina. He specializes in creating peerless custom waterscapes which work in harmony with your landscape, just as if mother nature had placed them there.

Art has a passion for creating exquisite water features and - as a perfectionist - a commitment to their exceptional performance and entrancing as well.

By utilizing high quality components and native materials - such as indigenous boulders, driftwood and aquatic plants - their craftsmen are able to create natural looking water features which are both beautiful and bold to last.

Whether you’re looking to add a koi pond, pondless waterfall, creek, stream or other water feature Western Carolina Waterscapes will exceed your expectations in bringing your vision to life.

Art Lynch and his team of exceptional craftsmen at Western Carolina Waterscapes are, quite simply, the best in the business - don’t settle for less.
